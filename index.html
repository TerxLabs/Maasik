About Us

In this era, where women are none less than men, we realized seeing the career responsibilities of women that includes preparing food for other, cleaning for others and carrying out personal hygiene for their children; have put a halt in taking caring of their personal health. Apart from all responsibilities, they too have to deal with menstruation cycle.

Noticing these feminine problems and the responsibility of taking care of their respective families, we have made an effort of minimizing their menace by working on creating products that might not relieve them of trouble but to a certain amount help them to be comfortable in their own skin.

We believe that, “Women’s health need to be front and centre - it’s often isn’t, but it needs to be.” A proper hygiene, sanitation is necessary for the women as she is the one who might not financially but socially helps to run the family. Keeping in mind all these facts, Massik Team has taken a move forward to cater to all the needs of women to live a healthy and happily life.
